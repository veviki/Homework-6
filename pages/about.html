Did you know facts about Africa foods?

Africa is a home to leafy greens, root vegetables, mashed tubers and beans, and many different plant crops across its lands. In Central and West Africa, traditional meals were often based on hearty vegetable soups and stews, full of spices and aromas, poured over boiled and mashed tubers and grains.

Africa cooking starts as a vegan base, packed with varieties of vegetables, and then the meat is added. Hence,vegetables are widely eaten and consumed through soups or stews. African also tend to consume more beans through a plethora of dishes such moi moi, rice and beans, akara, and varities of bean porridge

Africa dishes often incorporate a harmonious blend of savory, spicy, tangy, and earthy, flavors. Spices and herbs like ginger, garlic, chilies, cloves, coriander, and cumin are commonly used to create aropmatic and well-balanced dishes.
